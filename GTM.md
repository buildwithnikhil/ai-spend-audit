# Go-to-market (Credex AI Spend Audit)

## ICP

- Seed–Series B SaaS + AI-native startups ($500k–$15M ARR) buying multiple assistants simultaneously.
- Owners: CEO/CFO + RevOps + Eng leadership (budget friction).

## Channels

| Channel | Angle |
| --- | --- |
| Product Hunt | “Show HN style” transparency — publish methodology + pricing dataset changelog |
| Reddit (`/r/startups`, `/r/SaaS`, `/r/devops`) | Long-form comment breakdown of Cursor/Copilot overlap |
| X/Twitter | Before/after cards + quote tweets tagging vendors (neutral tone) |
| Engineering blogs | Technical teardown of JSON-driven audit engine |

## Reddit playbook

- Lead with **benchmark methodology**, not sales pitch.
- Offer to model anonymized stacks in-thread (screenshots → inbound).

## Product Hunt playbook

- Ship crisp OG visuals + live demo sandbox with prefilled “messy stack”.
- Schedule replies for first 4 hours; pin pricing dataset repo link.

## Twitter playbook

- Weekly “invoice confession” polls → link audit.
- Quote credible finance creators with savings deltas.

## First 100 users

1. 25 warm intros from portfolio peers.
2. 25 comment-led Reddit/X threads with tangible savings math.
3. 25 partner webinars (accelerators, fractional CFOs).
4. 25 PH + newsletter cascades (TLDR AI, Ben’s Bites swaps).

## Growth loops

1. Every share URL exposes “Run your own audit” CTA.
2. Email capture triggers Resend drip highlighting dataset updates when vendors move pricing.
